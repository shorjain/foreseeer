[01] Methodology & Logic

Understand the Models

Holt’s Linear Trend

Calculates both the average level and the daily "slope." Ideal for businesses currently in a growth or contraction phase.

Exponential Smoothing

Gives exponentially more weight to recent sales than older ones. Best for stable markets with minor fluctuations.

Moving Average

Smoothing technique that averages the last X days. Good for high-noise data, but lags behind sudden trend shifts.

⚠️ Critical Caveats

Models use historical data only. They cannot predict external "shocks" like weather events, competitor openings, or sudden viral trends.
